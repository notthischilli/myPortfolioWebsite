When I was first learning React JS I wanted to try out React Native and see the similarities between them. After I tried it, I wanted to try creating something with it, something simple but something that might come in handy for me to use. At that time I was searching for a Planning app but most were either day-to-day to-do app or something that didn't match up to what I was searching for. So, I decided to create an app that was more of a long term planning app that would let you create a guideline for the bigger overall goal that you wanted to achieve in a year.
