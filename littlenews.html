LITTLENEWS

Technologies Used
A little news site scrapper.
I wanted to try scrapping the internet for contents so I tried using cheerio JS and pupeeteer JS. The deployment of the app was the most frustrating part, as I spent 3 days trying to figure out why pupeteerJS wasn't working properly. But I'm glad I kept at it and managed to solve the problem. When the user goes to the scrap link, the site starts scrapping from the BBC website, the articles can be saved in MongoDB, deleted later and using saved reference the article will be fetched from the source again. It does take some time though. The site is deployed @ render.
